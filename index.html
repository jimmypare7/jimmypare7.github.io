Here are a couple of images of some of the cutting boards I have made. Recently, I have been experimenting with 'chaos boards', which are end grain cutting boards with chaotic patterns. I like to make end grain cutting boards because of the improved durability and self repairing properties.

Epoxy River Tables

As of September 2025, I have made 3 epoxy river tables. An epoxy river table was one of the first projects I took on in woodworking because they don't require as many tools as other projects. I outsourced the flattening process, and made a track saw jig for my circular saw to cut the slabs to size and make the mold for the epoxy pour.
