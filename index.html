School Projects

Here are some of my projects I have worked on at UVM.

Mechatronics Car

Autonomous robotic car designed to sort plastic bottles in an unknown course using an ultrasonic sensor and passive mechanisms.

Speed of Light Experiment

I measured the speed of light with 1.9% error.

Recurve Bow Adaptive Meshing FEA

Static analysis of a recurve bow in Abaqus using an adaptive remeshing process.

Finite Element Analysis of Drone Bracket

I performed FEA and hand calculations on a drone bracket I designed at the end of my sophomore year at UVM.

Compressible Flow Python Simulation

I used the Rusanov numerical method for a compressible flow simulation in Python.
